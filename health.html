Health

Health

At Bio Agro Energy Private Limited, we prioritize the health and well-being of the communities where we operate. Our CSR initiatives in health include:

  • Healthcare Access: Providing access to quality healthcare services by organizing medical camps, offering free health check-ups, and supporting local health centers with necessary medical equipment and supplies.
  • Health Awareness: Conducting health awareness programs to educate communities about preventive healthcare, hygiene, nutrition, and disease prevention.
  • Maternal and Child Health: Focusing on maternal and child health by supporting prenatal and postnatal care, immunization drives, and nutrition programs for mothers and children.

What We Do

Bio Agro Energy specializes in the production of ethanol through the fermentation of organic materials, primarily sourced from locally-grown, sustainable crops. Our state-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge technology ensure that our ethanol is of the highest quality, meeting rigorous industry standards. We supply ethanol for a variety of uses, including fuel for vehicles, industrial applications, and as a critical ingredient in the production of beverages and personal care products.
